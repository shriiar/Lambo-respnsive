FASHION
LAMBORGHINI

Fashion means something that is trendy or valued in a certain time period. The prevailing fashion/ trends/style is called ‘vogue’. Fashion can also simply mean our lifestyle: the clothing and accessories that we wear and the cosmetics that we apply. Besides clothing, ornaments, accessories, and make up, it also includes our mannerism and behavior. The way we talk, the words that we use while talking, the voice tone, the clothing, the way we wear the clothes, etc. are all aspects of fashion.
